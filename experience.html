Key Lessons Learned

  • Not your keys, not your coins (Mt. Gox, FTX)
  • Smart contract risk is real (countless protocol hacks)
  • Yield usually comes with hidden risk (Luna/UST)
  • Custody and security are paramount
  • Diversification across chains and strategies
  • Tax planning is crucial from day one
